How to Contact Cash.App Support

Your one-stop guide for troubleshooting, FAQs, and contacting Cash.App support to resolve any issues efficiently.

Cash.App Support

Cash.App simplifies sending money, paying bills, and managing finances. However, issues can occur. This guide helps you contact Cash.App support to resolve your concerns quickly.

1. Contacting Support Through the Mobile App

Follow these steps to get help via the Cash.App mobile app:

  • Open the Cash.App and tap on the profile icon.
  • Scroll down and select Support.
  • Choose your issue or tap Something Else to explain your problem.
  • If needed, select Contact Support to start a chat.

2. Support Through the Official Website

Visit the official Cash.App Help Center on the web:

  • Go to the Cash.App Help Center website.
  • Use the search bar to find articles about your issue.
  • If unresolved, scroll down to Contact Support for further help.

3. Using Social Media

Reach out to Cash.App on Twitter and Facebook:

  • On Twitter, tweet or send a direct message to the official Cash.App support handle.
  • On Facebook, visit the official Cash.App page and send a private message with your issue.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my Cash.App PIN?

  1. Tap on the "Profile" icon.
  2. Navigate to Security and select Change PIN.
  3. Follow the instructions to reset your PIN.
